Who is Avikus?

Welcome aboard the very first issue of the Avikus Newsletter. In this inaugural edition we introduce who we are, where our name comes from, and the technologies with which we are reshaping the way ships are sailed and controlled.

01 Who We Are

Avikus is HD Hyundai’s dedicated subsidiary for autonomous ship navigation. Born as the very first in-house venture of Hyundai Heavy Industries, it was established as a wholly owned (100%) subsidiary of HD Hyundai.

That pioneering spirit is written into our very name. Avikus traces back to “Avviker”, a root of the word “Viking” — our resolve to stand at the frontier of autonomous navigation. “Avviker” is also said to mean “to depart”, evoking those who left their homeland for the open sea. In that sense the name embodies, all at once, the spirit of explorers venturing into uncharted waters for the very first time.

OUR SLOGAN
“Maritime Autonomous Pioneer”

Through AI-based obstacle detection, collision avoidance, and automatic berthing assistance, Avikus is innovating the way vessels are navigated and controlled — in pursuit of a future where safety, enjoyment, and accessibility are within reach for everyone.

02 Our Products — HiNAS

HiNAS — the Hyundai intelligent Navigation Assistant System — is a family of AI-driven solutions that raise both safety and fuel efficiency, from the bridge to the shore office.

NAV
HiNAS Navigation — Camera-based navigation assistant

Fuses vision AI, radar, and AIS into a single augmented-reality (AR) view of the waters ahead, so the crew can recognise vessels and obstacles at a glance and keep full situational awareness on watch.

Key features   Sensor-fusion AR display  ·  AI obstacle recognition  ·  Retrofit-ready in ~3 days at port, no dry-docking
SVM
HiNAS SVM — 360° surround-view monitoring

Stitches multiple hull cameras into one 360-degree top-down view, removing blind spots for safer berthing, tugging, and close-quarters manoeuvring.

Key features   Surround & side view  ·  AI target detection beyond radar/AIS  ·  Onboard recording up to 30 days  ·  Scalable 4–10 cameras
CONTROL
HiNAS Control — Level 2 autonomous navigation

The world’s first commercialised IMO Level 2 solution. It reads surrounding traffic and directly steers heading and RPM to hold the planned route, avoid collisions, and optimise speed — while a certified officer stays in command and can override instantly.

Key features   Automatic route following  ·  Collision avoidance via course & speed control  ·  AI RPM optimisation for 3–8% fuel savings (independently verified)  ·  Sensor fusion (IR thermal, radar, AIS)  ·  DNV Type Approval
CLOUD
HiNAS Cloud — Cloud fleet management & analytics

A web-based shore platform that connects to the onboard HiNAS systems, giving fleet and safety teams near-real-time monitoring and full voyage playback from anywhere in the world.

Key features   Live fleet video with AIS & VDR data  ·  Automatic flagging of safety events  ·  Weather, ocean & port-call records  ·  24/7 recording with downloadable playback
